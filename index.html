HMS Pinafore — State Opera of South Australia, 2023

“Jeremy Kleeman's wit, energy and constant focus power his performance. He can act, he can move and he can sing. From his surprise first appearance, when he declares “I am the captain of the Pinafore”, his vocal worth is evident, with clear, precise articulation and warmly resonant tone. His Act 2 opener, “Fair moon, to thee I sing”, shows off his excellent legato line. Kleeman is a very fine artist” — Pat Wilson, Glam Adelaide
